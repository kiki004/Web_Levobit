Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences.

2. What cookies we use

Levobit uses only strictly necessary cookies that are essential for the operation of our contact form. Without these cookies, the contact form cannot function properly. We do not use any analytics or advertising cookies unless you accept them via our cookie banner (the banner only enables essential cookies by default).

3. List of cookies

  • cookie_consent_accepted – stores your consent preference (expires after 1 year).
  • PHPSESSID – used by our server to manage session data (contact form submission). Expires when you close your browser.

4. Third-party cookies

We do not currently embed third-party services (e.g., YouTube, Google Maps) that set their own cookies. If we add such features in the future, this policy will be updated.

5. How to control cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may break the contact form functionality.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about our cookie usage, contact us at office@levobit.ro.
